Frequently Asked Questions

How do I contact Facebook support directly?

You can contact Facebook support by calling our customer care number at +1 (888) 987-9070 or by visiting the Facebook Help Center and using the "Report a Problem" feature. For business accounts, additional support options are available in your Business Manager settings.

Why was my account disabled and how do I recover it?

Accounts are typically disabled for violating Facebook's Community Standards. To appeal, visit facebook.com/help and follow the account disabled instructions. You'll need to provide identification to verify your account. The review process may take several days.

How can I increase my account security?

We recommend: 1) Enabling two-factor authentication, 2) Using a strong, unique password, 3) Being cautious of suspicious messages, 4) Regularly checking active sessions, and 5) Keeping your contact information up to date. All these options are available in your Security Settings.

What should I do if I see fake accounts impersonating me?

Go to the fake profile, click the three dots (...) on the cover photo, and select "Find support or report profile". Choose "Pretending to be someone" and select your name. Facebook will review the report typically within 24 hours.

How do I manage what I see in my News Feed?

You can prioritize who to see first by visiting their profile and selecting "Favorites" or "See First" in the Following dropdown. You can also unfollow people without unfriending them, or adjust your feed preferences in Settings.
